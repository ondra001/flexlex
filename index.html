FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about FlexLex.

Is FlexLex really free?

Yes. The free version includes flashcards, write mode, spaced repetition, up to 2 sets with 30 words each, and full offline support. No account required, no ads. Pro unlocks all 8 study modes, unlimited sets and words, import tools, translation suggestions, spell check, and much more — for $10/year or $30 lifetime.

Does it work without internet?

100%. FlexLex stores everything locally on your device. No cloud sync, no server dependency. Your data stays on your phone, always available, even on a plane or in a remote area.

What languages does FlexLex support?

FlexLex is language-agnostic. You create your own word pairs, so it works with any language combination — English-Japanese, Spanish-French, or even technical terminology. The built-in dictionary suggestions cover dozens of popular languages.

How does spaced repetition work?

Every time you review a word, FlexLex records whether you knew it, found it hard, or didn't know it. Based on your answers, it calculates the optimal time to show that word again — right before you'd forget it. Words you struggle with appear more frequently; words you know well are spaced further apart.

Can I import my existing word lists?

Yes. FlexLex supports importing from Excel (.xlsx) files and plain text files. You can also use the OCR camera scanner (Pro) to photograph printed word lists, textbook pages, or whiteboards and convert them to flashcards instantly.

What are the pricing options?

FlexLex Pro is available as a $10/year subscription or a $30 one-time lifetime purchase. Both unlock every feature, including all future updates. The yearly plan can be cancelled anytime. If it lapses, you get a 7-day grace period before Pro features lock.
